Here is a short story about Brannt's adventures:

 Brannt starts his little adventure off in a small town of Colorado.
He lives there until he is somehow transported to Kalispell, Montana.
What a weird place! Why are the mountains so far away?
In this new place, Brannt learned to ride a bike, read and write, and make new friends.
As he grew older, he studied hard to make his dream come true.
He wanted to become a game designer!
When he became of age, he applyed to the Univercity of Montana.
Once he got in, he took a coding class and really liked it!
Then he started writing a short story. The rest is in the future...
